Exploring an extreme, will photography then disappear to make way for painting? What's the minimum left to take a photograph? The answer here is an illuminated ground, which stretches from our feet to the distant dark where we can disappear. The Outside, the Night. Then everything opened up with views of the sea, all subjects became possible, the notion of series disappeared, the images became singular before starting to assemble into diptychs, triptychs and other pieces.
